Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive foccacia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cramberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the Focaccia:
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
For the topping:
  • 3 medium yellow onions,thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 once brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife id desired, thoungh it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle atthachment mix togher all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed
